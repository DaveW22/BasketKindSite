What this policy covers

This policy applies to the Kind Basket website and mobile app. It explains what personal data we collect, how we use it, your rights, and how to contact us.

Personal data we may collect

  • Account details (name, email, password)
  • Contact details
  • Shopping lists and preferences
  • Device/app usage data
  • Location or postcode (if used for local pricing)
  • Receipt data (if receipt scanning is enabled)
  • Customer support messages

How we collect data

We collect data you provide directly (e.g. when you create an account, contact support, or use app features) and data collected automatically (e.g. device type, usage, cookies, analytics).

Why we use your data

  • To provide and improve Kind Basket
  • To personalise your experience
  • To communicate with you
  • To comply with legal obligations
  • To protect our users and service

Lawful bases under UK GDPR

We process your data under lawful bases including consent, contract, legal obligation, and legitimate interests.

Receipt scanning and anonymised pricing insights

If you use receipt scanning, we process receipt data to provide pricing insights. Data may be anonymised and aggregated for analytics.

Analytics and product improvement

We use analytics to improve our product and understand usage patterns. Data is anonymised where possible.

Marketing communications and consent

We only send marketing communications if you have given consent. You can opt out at any time.

Sharing data with service providers

We may share data with trusted service providers who help us operate our service. They are required to protect your data and only use it for specified purposes.

Retailer, affiliate and partner integrations

We may share data with retailers or partners if you use integrations. This will be clearly explained and subject to your consent.

International transfers and safeguards

Your data may be transferred outside the UK/EEA with appropriate safeguards in place.

Data retention

We retain your data only as long as necessary for the purposes described. You can request deletion at any time.

Security

We use technical and organisational measures to protect your data from unauthorised access, loss, or misuse.

Children’s privacy

Our service is not intended for children under 16. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

Your UK GDPR rights

  • Right to access your data
  • Right to rectification
  • Right to erasure (“right to be forgotten”)
  • Right to restrict processing
  • Right to data portability
  • Right to object
  • Right to withdraw consent

Right to complain to the ICO

You have the right to complain to the UK Information Commissioner’s Office (ICO) if you believe your data has been mishandled.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Significant changes will be communicated clearly.
