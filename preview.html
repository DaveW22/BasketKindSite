Compare UK supermarket prices before you shop.

Kind Basket is being built to make grocery price comparison in the UK genuinely useful. Whether you want to find the cheapest supermarket for your basket, compare Tesco, Aldi, Lidl and other UK supermarkets, or simply reduce what you spend on your weekly shop, Kind Basket aims to give you a clearer picture before you reach the checkout.

Most households want to save money on groceries but do not have time to check prices manually across multiple stores. Kind Basket is designed to close that gap — turning your reusable shopping list into a live weekly shop savings estimate, updated as price data becomes available.
